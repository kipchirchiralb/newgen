Our Services

GNSS Surveying & Controls.

GPS control networks, both horizontal and vertical, tied to the Kenyan geodetic reference system through static GNSS surveying.

Cadastral Surveys.

New grant Surveys, Subdivision surveys (both urban and rural), beacon and boundary re-establishment surveys. These types of surveys are done to support title (ownership) registration for property or property boundary re-establishment in accordance with the Survey Act (Cap 299, Laws of Kenya).

Topographical Survey.

Survey of natural and man-made features in any given area with height & coordinate information tied to Survey of Kenya datum through GNSS (Static & RTK) and Drone survey methods. The end product is usually a plan showing all significant features including elevations in the form of spot heights or contours and any other spatial data requested. The data is then delivered as hard copy (paper or film) and soft copies in shape files, PDF, DWG, DXF, GPX and KML formats, and all work is archived for future reference.

Engineering, Construction Support Surveys

Site surveying for design work, setting out and carrying out as built surveys. A site survey is used to collect data about the topography of a site and is presented as a plan or drawing. The purpose of such surveys is for conducting setting out works. Setting out involves extraction of data from a drawing and accurately marking out the drawing features on the ground. This can be electricity power supply lines, earth works, sewers, road works, bridges, buildings or housing development.
